3. Every field's own label→field gap (universal consistency check)
Independent of row-sharing: for every one of the 25 distinct .fr fields in both modals, across all 4 #mov/#btcov states and both viewports (50 measurements total), does the label's own bottom edge sit the same distance from its own field's top edge? All 50 measurements returned exactly 4.000px — this is .fr label{margin-bottom:.25rem} (a single shared rule, unscoped, un-overridden anywhere), so the gap is structurally guaranteed rather than coincidental.
